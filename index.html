Welcome to Surf Simulator!

Learn to ride the waves like a pro in this enhanced surfing experience!

Basic Controls

Press SPACE to paddle and build power.

When your paddle power is over 50%, press UP to catch a wave!

Riding the Wave

Use LEFT and RIGHT arrows to maintain your balance.

Press SPACE while riding to jump!

Tricks & Combos

While in the air, press F to perform a basic trick.

When your trick meter is full, press G for an advanced trick!

Chain tricks to build your combo multiplier!

Collectibles & Obstacles

Collect shells for extra points!

Grab power-ups for special abilities!

Avoid obstacles like rocks and other surfers!
